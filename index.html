This weeks Top Three...

Each week we choose 3 winning recipes which we think you should try to cook at home!

Beef Ragu Pasta Bake
Our Food Optimised version of the much-loved Italian pasta dish is destined to be a dinner time regular in your home.

Ingredients

  • Low calorie cooking spray
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 celery sticks, finely chopped
  • 1 large carrot, peeled and finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 250g chestnut mushrooms, finely chopped
  • 500g lean beef mince (5% fat or less)
  • 700g jar passata
  • 8 tbsp tomato purée
  • 250ml beef stock
  • 1 tsp dried mixed herbs
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 400g dried conchiglioni
  • A handful of fresh basil leaves, finely chopped
  • 200g mozzarella, cubed
